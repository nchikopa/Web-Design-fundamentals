Tuna, Spinach and Mushroom Pie
Serves 6

2 x 170g cans Lucy Star Tuna Chunks, well drained
4 rashers rindless streaky bacon, chopped
50g butter
250g black or brown mushrooms, sliced
1 bunch spinach, washed, drained and shredded
125ml cream
salt and miled black pepper
3-4 potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
olive oil

Fry the bacon in the butter in a wide frying pan until cooked. Add the mushroom, cover and cook for 1 to 2 minutes until tender. Add the spinach, cover and steam for 2 minutes until limp. Stir everything together. Pour in the cream and season with salt and pepper. Boil uncovered until the sauce has reduced by half and thickened a little.

Heat the oven to 220oC. Spread the spinach and mushroom mixture into a 2-litre baking dish and top with the tuna, roughly broken into pieces. Arrange the potato slices evenly on top and grizzle with olive oil.

Place the banking dish in the oven and turn on the oven griller. Bake the pie for 10 to 15 minutes until piping hot and the potatoes golden brown.
